Project Pitch

Our vision in two minutes

Your question. Answered now.

It's Sunday evening. The library is closed. Your assignment is due Monday. You need to know if KdG has access to a specific IEEE journal, and if not, how to request it through inter-library loan.

Old answer: send an email. Wait two days.
New answer: ask the helpdesk. Get it in under two seconds.

< 2s AI response
NL + EN bilingual
24/7 always on
  • Knows your library. Answers grounded in the real KdG FAQ, not the open internet
  • Escalates intelligently. Routes to a librarian only when it genuinely needs one
  • Librarians stay in control. Live dashboard, one-click pickup, Teams notifications
  • Built to last. Swap the AI model without touching the chat logic
